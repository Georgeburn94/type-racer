Instructions

Select Difficulty: Choose a difficulty level (Easy, Medium, or Hard) from the dropdown menu.

Start the Test: Click the "Start" button to begin the typing test. A new sentence will appear.

Type the Sentence: Type the sentence shown in the text box below. Your typing will be highlighted:

  • Blue for correct letters.
  • Red for incorrect letters.

No Backspace: You cannot use the backspace key to correct mistakes.

Stop the Test: Click the "Stop" button when you finish typing the sentence.

View Results: Your time and words per minute (WPM) score will be displayed in the results section.

Retry: Click the "Retry" button to start a new test.
